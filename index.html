A very simple sorter for Objectified. Pick the character categories you want to see included, then press Start.

Certain options have details that you can hover to read.

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them. (The less you pick 'tie', the clearer the results.)

If you select all 3 categories you typically get 300+ combinations. If you spend 5 seconds on each, that's 25 minutes, or more. Prepare to spend a little bit of time on this, in general.



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.



Based on, and copies most code from, this touhou sorter.
Takes assets from the official Objectified website and Pluto's lore guide. Character categories also come from its trello.
